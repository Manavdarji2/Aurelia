The Maison

Our Origin

Born from Reverence for Craft

AURELIA was founded in 1987 by Elena Moretti in a quiet studio overlooking the Navigli canals of Milan. She believed that true luxury is not about excess — it is about restraint, intention, and the sacred relationship between hand and material.

What began as a private atelier serving a discerning few has grown into a global symbol of quiet sophistication. Yet every piece still passes through the same hands, follows the same unhurried rhythm, and honors the same founding principle: nothing leaves our workshop until it is extraordinary.

Philosophy

Materials as Language

We source Italian silk from century-old mills in Como, cashmere from the highlands of Inner Mongolia, and leather from family-owned tanneries in Tuscany. Each material is chosen not for its rarity alone, but for the story it tells against the skin.

Our design philosophy is one of subtraction — removing everything that doesn't serve the wearer. What remains is pure, purposeful, and deeply personal.

Our Journey

Milestones

1987

Elena Moretti opens the first AURELIA atelier on Via Savona, Milan. A private studio creating bespoke pieces for a circle of connoisseurs.

1995

The first ready-to-wear collection debuts at Milan Fashion Week. Critics call it "the opposite of everything loud in fashion."

2004

AURELIA opens its flagship on Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré, Paris. The interior is designed entirely in obsidian marble and hand-forged brass.

2018

Launch of the Sustainability Atelier — a commitment to zero-waste pattern cutting and exclusively natural, traceable materials.

2026

The digital maison launches — bringing the AURELIA experience to clients worldwide while preserving the intimacy of our craft.
